A/B Testing Value Proposition.

I believe in learning and constant improvement - especially when it comes to refining the value proposition.

In this example, customer research helped inform the new value proposition leading to a 24% increase in conversions at 95% statistical significance. Exit Intent Poll showed that the landing page didn’t explain the service and the benefits clearly. The explainer video wasn’t helping - page visitors bounced before they got to it or scrolled right past it. We created a new version of the copy above the fold with a clear value-based headline and scannable benefits, and launched an A/B test. The CEO couldn’t hold back his excitement when he found out about the 24% lift in conversions.
